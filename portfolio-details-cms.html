Campus Management System

Project information

  • Project: Campus Management System
  • Client: University of Baltistan, Skardu
  • Project date: August 2019 - Till Date
  • Project URL: https://uobs.edu.pk

This project involved developing and maintaining a comprehensive Campus Management System (CMS) for the University of Baltistan. Key modules included Learning Management, HR, Payroll, Admissions, and Fee Management Systems. Utilizing Laravel, Vue.js, Tailwind CSS, and MySQL, the CMS streamlined administrative processes and enhanced operational efficiency across the institution. Docker was used for deployment, ensuring scalability and easy maintenance.
