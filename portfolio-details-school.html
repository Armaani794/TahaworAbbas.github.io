School Management System

Project information

  • Category: School Management System
  • Client: Multiple Educational Institutions
  • Project date: January 2020 ~ Till Date
  • Implemented in: 25+ Schools

Designed and developed a product-based School Management System (SMS) tailored to meet the needs of diverse educational institutions. The SMS includes key modules such as student enrollment, attendance tracking, grade management, fee collection, and teacher-student communication. The system is built using Laravel, Vue.js, and MySQL, ensuring scalability, security, and ease of use. It has been successfully implemented in over 25 schools, streamlining their administrative processes and improving overall efficiency.
